# 2. Basic Concepts

**Stream:** potentially **infinite** sequence; limited RAM → algorithms use **summaries**, **sketches**, or **forgetting**.

**Sliding window:** statistics computed only over the **last W** elements.

**Exponential decay:** older events contribute **less** than recent ones (smooth forgetting).

**Sampling:** draw a representative subset without storing everything (e.g. reservoir sampling — advanced).

**Concept drift:** the data-generating process changes; models must **adapt** or **detect** change.
